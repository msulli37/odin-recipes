What is a BEC?

Bacon Egg and Cheese (BEC, for short) are an easy breakfast sandwich to make, and -with the right technique- can create a saucy and crunchy amalgamation of the short list of ingredients. When I started making these, I quickly found myself making them everyday!

The Process

    Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
    Steps
  1. Cook bacon to your liking. Three pieces should be enough for one person.
  2. Butter pan, and add 2-3 eggs. Be careful not to break the yolk!
  3. While eggs are cooking, toast bread if you have a toaster.
  4. Cook eggs just until the egg whites are done, then immediately turn heat off.
  5. Add cheese on top of eggs, break yolk and spread over cooked egg whites.
  6. Grab bread/toast, add eggs, then add bacon.
