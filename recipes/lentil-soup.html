Lentil Soup

A delicious looking bowl of lentil soup! Image and alternative recipe can be found here!

Lentil soup is an easy, healthy, and delicious option for anyone looking to make a meal for multiple people, or maybe to meal prep for a busy week to come. I personally love making lentil soup because it gives me energy during my week, and I enjoy experiementing with various spices to create a unique flavor each time I make it. I hope that in sharing my recipe with y'all that more people grow to love this dish!
