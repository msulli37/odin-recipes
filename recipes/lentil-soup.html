Lentil Soup

A delicious looking bowl of lentil soup! Image and alternative recipe can be found here!

Lentil soup is an easy, healthy, and delicious option for anyone looking to make a meal for multiple people, or maybe to meal prep for a busy week to come. I personally love making lentil soup because it gives me energy during my week, and I enjoy experiementing with various spices to create a unique flavor each time I make it. I hope that in sharing my recipe with y'all that more people grow to love this dish!

    Ingredients
  • 3 Onions
  • 2 Large Carrots
  • 5 Celery Stocks
  • 16 oz. bag of Lentils
  • Peeled Garlic Cloves
  • 3 containers of vegetable broth
    Spices
  • Chili Powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Siracha Seasoning
  • Tumeric
  • Salt
    Steps:
  1. Put lentils in a bowl/pot of water to soak
  2. Clean celery and peel carrot skin
  3. Dice onions, heres a tutorial for those who need it!
  4. Chop carrots and celery
  5. Put enough vegetable oil to cover bottom of a large pot
  6. Turn stove heat up to medium
  7. Add chopped onions and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring often
  8. When onions are browning, add carrots and cook for ~3 minutes
  9. Add celery and let cook for ~2 minutes
  10. If you have a garlic press: press around 12 cloves of garlic into dish and stir frequently
  11. If you don't have a garlic press, cut the cloves into fine pieces, add them to the dish and stir frequently
  12. Cook garlic for ~30 seconds. Be careful not to overcook!
  13. Add all containers of vegetable broth, and turn heat up to max
  14. Remember to stir occasionally
  15. Strain soaked lentils
  16. Bring broth to a boil, then add lentils
  17. Cook soup at this heat (with a cover for pot if avaiable) for a minimum of 30 minutes, and remember to stir often
  18. After at least 30 minutes, turn heat off and let soup cool down
  19. Once soup is not smoking hot, add desired seasoning
  20. Test soup for taste, and add seasoning until it tastes yummy!
