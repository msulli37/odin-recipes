Egg Rice

Homepage

It doesn't get easier than egg rice! And it's quite delicious

Egg rice is another quick meal that can function as a breakfast, lunch, snack, or even as a part of a dinner! It takes less than 5 minutes to make, and with the right combination of ingredients tastes amazing. It's can be a good pick me up when you don't have the energy to make something more demanding, or maybe just a side to a larger meal. A simple meal makes a versatile meal, so having this in your recipe book will help you out eventually!

    Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Seasame oil
    Steps:
  1. Cook rice! I like to batch prepare my rice ahead of time, which cuts down on the prep time for this meal.
  2. Cook 1 to 3 eggs (depending on how hungry you are).
  3. If using preprepped rice, heat up in microwave for a minute.
  4. Add a tablespoon or two of soy sauce to rice
  5. Add a teaspoon or two of seasame oil to rice
  6. Add egg(s) to rice
  7. Mix altogether!
